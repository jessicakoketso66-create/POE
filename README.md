## Project Overview
-	Name :Butter and Bliss 
•	History :Butter and Bliss originated from a small kitchen ,driven by the passion for baking and creating sweet treats for family and friends ,as demand grew the business grew and we turned it into a local bakery known for its fresh and quality goodies .
•	Vision :To become a leading bakery known for high-quality ,delicious cakes that bring joy to every celebration 
•	Mission : To provide freshly baked ,affordable and creatively designed cakes while delivering excellent customer service and maintaining high standards of quality and hygiene .
•	Target Audience : Everyone is our customer especially when they have a sweet-tooth .

## Website Goals and Objectives
-Goals:
•	Promote the bakery, attract more customers and increase sales by showcasing product and services online .
-Objectives :
•	The website should display the bakery’s menu and prices clearly ,allow customers to place orders or make enquiries online ,provide contact details and location ,showcase image of cakes and pastries and build the brands reputation through a user-friendly design /website .
